Gluten intolerance

You may be up to 7 times more likely to develop gluten intolerance than people who have a CC genotype. You may consider limiting consumption of grains that contain gluten – e.g., wheat, rye, and barley. However, it is important to note that only 1% of the population is gluten intolerant, and there are many factors that can affect your digestion, beyond a single gene.
