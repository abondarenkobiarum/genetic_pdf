HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT

Your genetic information is displayed in three different ways, using graphs and imagery to help you understand what it means and how it affects you.

SINGLE GENETIC MARKERS

Single Genetic Markers represent your genotype for a particular genetic trait. For each genetic trait, you inherit one version, or allele, from each of your parents. The paired alleles in this graphic are color-coded to indicate your genotype.

Yellow represents alternate allele
Green represents typical allele

GENETIC Data TABLE

The Genetic Data Table shows your genotype for each gene, which is identified by its rsID number. Your genotype is characterized by an allele combination. The alternate allele is the allele that has an effect on a genetic score such as GoalGetter calculates it. In some cases, Helix may not have been able to determine your genotype for a particular SNP, and a "--" appears in the table.
