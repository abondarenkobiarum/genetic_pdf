Your genes are the map.
YOUR BLOOD IS THE TRAFFIC.

Your Genetic Potential Summary (GPS™) tells
you how to get to where you want to go.

InsideTracker scientists have analyzed your unique GPS™ across five important categories: Weight, Sleep, Healthy Aging, Performance, and Sensitivities.

Here’s how we do it:
1
Our team analyzes current research for 261 genetic markers and compares it to your personal genetic data, in order to determine your genetic potential for single genetic traits (like lactose intolerance) as well as broad groups of traits (like athletic power and endurance).
2
We’ve used your Helix results, which include 261 of the 261 genetic markers we analyze. Every DNA test covers a specific set of genetic markers; due to processing errors and other unforseen technical circumstances, it is possible that not every marker will be sequenced. Our algorithm includes adjustments for the missing markers to make our analysis of your results as strong as possible.
3
We then compare your genetic potential to your biological data and your personal preferences for nutrition, exercise and lifestyle... Based on the combined view of your genetic potential, your biological data, and your personal preferences and habits, we provide you with action steps to take towards achieving – or avoiding – your body’s potential for certain traits.
